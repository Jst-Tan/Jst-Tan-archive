Heroes Everywhere (French)

(PJ Masks: Power Heroes Season 1 Episode 1)

Heroes Everywhere is the first episode of PJ Masks: Power Heroes Season 1. It featured the new addition of Ice Cub into the team, the new headquarter and much more, however it do not featured the new transformation song. It is an exciting episode to be honest and I hope everyone enjoy it.

PJ Masks: Power Heroes is the new reboot of PJ Masks, but also known as the sixth season in production. PJ Masks: Power Heroes feature the expansion of 3 characters in PJ Masks into a team of 9, which is called Power Heroes, new theme, transformation and credit song, new character, new location, new vehicles, awesome new invention and much more.

We currently have a new Discord server for everyone, including all PJ Masks and Power Heroes fan, join the server here: https://discord.gg/cBDVPexFQA. For more information, please check out my post about PJ Masks: Power Heroes here: https://jsttan.substack.com/p/pj-masks-power-heroes.
