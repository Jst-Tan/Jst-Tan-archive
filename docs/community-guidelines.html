Community Guidelines

Jst Tan currently has a complex community guidelines. These guidelines is used across all communities of Jst Tan, with some guidelines optional for some of my community to follow.

  • Keep in TOPIC
    Please keep the discussion on the main topic of the respective channel. Each channel is meant for a topic of discussion and please respect the layout and topic of each channel. If you are going off-topic in the channel, all messages will be deleted.
  • No spam
    All members shouldn't attempt or try to spam this server in any method, like sending repeated messages, images, GIFs, videos and much more. All repeated messages over 3 times are considered spam. Sex images aren't allowed too!
  • Languages
    Currently, there are three languages allowed inside this server, which are English, Malay and Simplified Chinese with more languages allowed soon. All other languages aren't allowed on this server for now. Fewer languages mean everyone is easier to understand and moderate.
  • Excessive Languages
    Any sort of excessive language is NOT ALLOWED and wouldn't be tolerated under any and all situations and circumstances. Example of excessive language is rude, bad words, harassment and many stuff that would be considered excessive.
  • No Hate, No Bullying
    Any sort of hate and bullying are hereby not allowed! Hate against anyone on this server and bullying against any members in or off this server is NOT ALLOWED. Bullying including group harassment against a specific person while hate including expressing hate, boycotting or personal criticising against a person.
  • Be civil and behave properly
    All members are required to be civil and behave properly. All members are required to respect each other in the server, no matter religion, nationality, opinion and sex. Everyone should be treated nicely and shouldn't be treated rudely. No one should be treated badly. Treat others how you want to be treated.
  • No trolling
    All members shouldn't attempt to troll anyone in and outside of this server. Do not try to troll any members in or outside of the server, including harassing others, faking as others and much more. Any sort of trolling, from faking as other username or profile picture.
  • No adult content (NSFW)
    All members shouldn't try to share any adult or NSFW content. All content shared inside this server should be family-friendly. This is a family-friendly server, so say NO to adult content by all means.
  • Respect everyone's privacy
    All members are required to respect each and everyone's privacy under any and all circumstances and situations. Everyone should have their own independent rights to their privacy and we do not allow members to violate others' privacy under all means unless requested by law enforcement for possibly illegal actions.
  • Respect server management
    All members in this server should respect server management. All announcements and changes made by the team management will be approved by the highest management, and all new rules will be enforced accordingly. In any situation, admin have the rights to said the last word and decide on the matter, they had proven their worth anyway. Members are required to respect the server management team in all situations.
  • No racism/racist
    All members shouldn't be racism and racist against anyone inside this server, no matter their race, nationality, religion, or occupation under any and all circumstances. All members should be treated fairly and should be treated the same.
