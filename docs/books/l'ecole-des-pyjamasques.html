L'ecole Des Pyjamasques

It's night, yet Pyjamasques go to school. And, it's a very strange class, a class full of little nocturnal heroes! Are present the Mascrapules, Lilifée and Tatouro. Professor Energuman begins by showing them gadgets. At that moment, comes Roméo who wants to transform the school to make his laboratory! Fortunately, Les Pyjamasques and Les Mascrapules team up and despite their differences, manage to counter Roméo.

This ebook is being created by Jst Tan, scanned from hardcover books of Les Pyjamasques. It is an awesome French books, and it is available to be sale in French only, so I share a free ebook copy here for everyone to check it out. I do not plan to do anything with it, except sharing it with everyone. I hope everyone enjoyed it.
